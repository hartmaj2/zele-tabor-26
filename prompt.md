## Game description

### Motivation

We want to prepare for the final battle against the red queen, we will do this by infiltrating castle of the red queen. 

### What to do

To do this, we will have to pass through border control of the red queen. So the kids will have to pretend that they are workers from the castle

### How to do it

The kids will have templates of passports that every incoming person has to have when they enter the castle (the queen is a control freak). On the passport, there will already be prefilled, what they have to pretend.

Maybe they will have to have big nose, super long hair, big red pimple somewhere, big ears, big teeth, scar, missing eye and so on.

They will also have not only physical traits but mental traits like stammer, schizophrenia, mania, depression.

If the kids don't really match the person they should be by the passport, the border control will reject them. 


### What to do once they get in

There will be around 5 stations inside the castle, where the kids will gain resources that they will be able to use for crafting items, which will then give them buffs or give the enemy army debuffs for the last battle against the red queen.

At each station they will be able to get one kind of resource. The resource should correspond to some kind of worker that works at the castle.

The resources will be just stickers which they will then stick on a grid the back of the passport. There will be limited amount of fields onto which they will be able to stick these stickers. Once the kids have filled their backpack (back of the passport), they will go back to their team base where they will be able to use the resources to craft some items.

One field of the grid will be used for the queens stamp. If kid doesn't have this stamp, they must have cheated the border control so they will be kicked out (possibly imprisoned).
